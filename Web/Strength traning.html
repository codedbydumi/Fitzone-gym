Strength Training: Tips for Beginners

Your guide to starting strength training safely and effectively.

Essential Tips for Beginners

1. Start with Bodyweight Exercises

Begin your journey with exercises like push-ups, squats, and lunges to build a solid foundation.

2. Focus on Form

Always prioritize proper form over lifting heavier weights to prevent injuries.

3. Create a Balanced Routine

Incorporate exercises that target all major muscle groups for a well-rounded approach.

4. Progress Gradually

Increase weights and intensity gradually to allow your body to adapt.

5. Stay Consistent

Commit to a regular schedule to see improvements in strength and endurance.
