Frequently Asked Questions

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel. A small administrative fee applies.

Are there any joining fees?

No joining fees for the first month! After that, there's a one-time enrollment fee of $50 for new members.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel with 30 days written notice. No cancellation fees for memberships held longer than 12 months.

Do you offer corporate memberships?

Yes! We offer special corporate rates for groups of 5 or more. Contact us for customized corporate wellness packages.
