Workout Tips

Tip 1: Warm Up Properly

Warming up prepares your body for exercise by increasing blood flow to your muscles.

Tip 2: Stay Hydrated

Drinking water before, during, and after your workout is crucial for optimal performance.

Tip 3: Focus on Form

Maintaining proper form helps prevent injuries and maximizes the effectiveness of your exercises.
