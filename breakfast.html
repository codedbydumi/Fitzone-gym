5 Healthy Breakfast Ideas for Busy Mornings

Quick, nutritious breakfast options to kickstart your day!

Breakfast Ideas

1. Overnight Oats

Combine rolled oats with yogurt or milk and your favorite toppings. Let it sit overnight for a quick, delicious breakfast.

2. Smoothie Bowl

Blend your favorite fruits with a bit of spinach and yogurt. Top with nuts, seeds, or granola for extra crunch.

3. Greek Yogurt Parfait

Layer Greek yogurt with fresh fruits and a sprinkle of granola for a satisfying breakfast.

4. Avocado Toast

Smashed avocado on whole-grain toast topped with a poached egg makes for a filling and nutritious breakfast.
